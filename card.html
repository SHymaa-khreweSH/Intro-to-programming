Blue Whale

blue whale is that it's the largest animal ever known to have lived on Earth, even larger than any dinosaur!
A blue whale's heart alone can weigh as much as a small car, and its arteries are so large that a human could crawl through them.

  • Scientific Name: Balaenoptera musculus
  • Average Length: 21 to 27 meters
  • Average Lifespan: 70 to 90 years
  • Habitat: oceans all around the world

The blue whale, the largest animal on Earth, can produce sounds reaching 188 decibels making it one of the loudest animals in the kingdom. Its heart weighs as much as a small car, and it can swim at speeds of up to 20 miles per hour despite its massive size!
