Barn Swallow

The swallow is a symbol of freedom and return, known for its remarkable migration patterns. Some swallow species return to the exact same nesting spots every year with impressive accuracy!

  • Scientific Name: Hirundo rustica.
  • Average Length: 15-20 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 4 years.
  • Habitat: Arabian Peninsula and other.

The barn swallow is a small, elegant bird with a forked tail and glossy blue-black plumage. It thrives in open habitats like farmland near water sources and is known for its agility in catching insects in mid-flight.
